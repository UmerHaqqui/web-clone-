UKPCCI meets Pubjab’s Chief Minister, Usman Buzdar

CM House, Lahore, Pakistan: UK Pakistan Chamber of Commerce and Industry met with and deliberated in a closed door session with the Chief Minister of Punjab and his executive team. The Chief Minister spoke of the anti-corruption measures, and the sheer investment potential of Pakistan’s most populous province, and encouraged the UKPCCI saying that his team was at their disposal to facilitate Foreign Direct Investment in Pakistan.

Discussing Pakistan with the Pakistan High Commission

Prime Minister’s Secretariat, Islamabad, Pakistan: UK Pakistan Chamber of Commerce and Industry met with Shehryar Afridi, Interior Minister, who briefed the delegation on matters pertaining to the interior ministry, and development. In turn, the UKPCCI updated the Interior Minister about the £200m investment trail, starting from Karachi, and ending in Islamabad. The interior minister expressed his delight, and arranged for a private dinner to honour the delegation at Islamabad Club.

UKPCCI meets Interior Minister ahead of Cabinet Reshuffle

Pakistan High Commission senior diplomats visited the UKPCCI directors are Fredos London for their upcoming trade delegation to Pakistan. His Excellency Mohammed Nafees Zakriya expressed support for various sectors, with an emphasis on Education and bleeding edge software.

UKPCCI meets Syed Zulfiqar Bukhari, Special Assistant to PM Khan

Prime Minister’s Secretariat, Islamabad, Pakistan: UK Pakistan Chamber of Commerce and Industry finalised it’s nation-wide tour in Islamabad, where they met with the Special Assistant to the Prime Minister for Overseas Pakistanis & Human Resource Development, Sayed Zulfiqar Abbas Bukhari.
