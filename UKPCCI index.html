“UK Pakistan Chamber of Commerce and Industry was established in 1984 with the aim of promoting trade and investment between the UK and Pakistan. Since then, has grown considerably to become a highly recognized entity in various business circles in both regions, as well as being recognized by the respective governments regarding efforts to boost trade.

UKPCCI provides excellent B2B networking opportunities for its members with high profile events in London. It arranges seminars, symposiums and workshops on emerging business techniques, business models, innovative ideas and e-commerce. The UKPCCI encourages empowerment and assists female entrepreneurs in participating in national and international economic activities through its platform. The organization welcomes businessmen, entrepreneurs and young professionals to make the UKPCCI a more vibrant body, and to meet emergent challenges in the constantly evolving business world.”

ABOUT UKPCCI

UKPCCI Was Established In 1979 And Has The Honour Of Being Recognised By The Governments Of The UK And Pakistan; It Is Growing Quickly With The Efforts Of Its Dedicated Members And Executive Committees.

The Board Of Directors Of The UKPCCI Consists Of Business Professionals From Various Sectors And Industries, Each Of Them Dedicated To Achieving The Central Objective Of Increasing Trade And Investment Between The UK And Pakistan. The Chamber Is Run By A Professional Team Of Directors, And Led By The Dedicated President; Regular Meetings Of The Members Take Place At Their Head Office In London.
